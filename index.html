About the XENON

Xavier Ng's Signature Monometal Yoyo

XENON Collection 2 – Upgraded for peak performance:

Wider Gap (+0.1mm): Smoother binds, less snag.

Heavier (+1g): More power, more stability, longer spins.

Shadow X & Mutant X: No bi-metal styling = ultimate stability (Xavier-approved).

Scarlet Violet: Sleek pink inner rims – Xavier’s top pick!

Forged from durable 6061 aluminium and refined with bimetal styling, the XENON is the signature yoyo of 3-time Singapore National Champion Xavier Ng.

Its rounded shape offers an exceptional comfort in hand, while its precise weight distribution ensures rock-solid stability every throw.

This yoyo was used to win 2024 Singapore Nationals, making it the ultimate monometal weapon for competitive players.

(Disclaimer: this is not a bimetal, the rims on this monometal are polished)
